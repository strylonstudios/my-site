### Media Sizing & Aspect Ratio Guidelines ###

Use this guide when creating or resizing images for the Strylon Studios website. The Tailwind `object-cover` class is used everywhere, meaning images will dynamically crop to fill their containers without stretching. However, providing images near these aspect ratios will produce the best visual results.

==================================================
1. Videos
==================================================
File: photos/common/hero_video.mp4
- Ideal Ratio: 16:9 (Standard Widescreen)
- Ideal Resolution: 1920x1080
- Note: This is a background video. Keep the file size under 10MB to ensure fast loading.

==================================================
2. Project & Gallery Images
==================================================
File: photos/photobooth/preview1.jpg
- Role: Main Photobooth Hero & Wide Gallery Banner
- Ideal Ratio: 16:9 (Widescreen)
- Ideal Resolution: 1920x1080 or 1200x675

File: photos/events/event1.jpg 
- Role: Highlight Gallery Focus Image (Takes up 2 rows)
- Ideal Ratio: 1:1 (Square) or 4:3 (Slightly Landscape)
- Ideal Resolution: 1080x1080 or 1200x900

File: photos/common/event1.jpg & photos/events/event_special.jpg
- Role: Smaller Gallery Grid Items
- Ideal Ratio: 1:1 (Square) or 4:3 (Standard Photo)
- Ideal Resolution: 800x800 or 1200x900

File: photos/project 2/project1.jpg
- Role: Featured Project Card & Wide Gallery Bottom Strip
- Ideal Ratio: 16:9 (Widescreen)
- Ideal Resolution: 1920x1080

==================================================
3. Team Profiles
==================================================
Files: sanjay.jpg, selva.jpg, rajkumar.jpg
- Role: Circular profile pictures in 'Our Team'
- Ideal Ratio: 1:1 (Perfect Square)
- Ideal Resolution: 400x400 or 500x500
- Note: Ensure the person's face is dead-center.

==================================================
4. Client Logos
==================================================
Files: clients/client1.svg to client6.svg (or PNGs)
- Role: Marquee Scroller Logo
- Ideal Ratio: Anywhere from 1:1 to 4:1 (Rectangular / Wide)
- Ideal Resolution: Max height 200px (e.g. 500x150)
- Note: MUST have a transparent background. Make sure the text is visible on the dark #0B0F1A background (use white/light versions of the logos).
