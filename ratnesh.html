About Me

As the CEO and founder of Strylon Studios, I've dedicated my career to creating cutting-edge game development and AR/VR experiences. My journey in the tech world began with a passion for interactive storytelling and a vision to bridge the gap between virtual worlds and reality.

With a strong foundation in Unity and C# development, I've successfully led multiple projects from concept to completion, including Delphi TVS, Yogam, AR Alphabet, AR Medical, and Photobooth. My team and I focus on creating memorable user experiences and pushing technological boundaries.

My expertise in artificial intelligence and NLP has allowed me to develop intelligent systems including offline natural language processing, speech-to-text, and text-to-speech for character interaction within our applications. I'm also experienced with Linux systems, custom Android ROMs, and various development tools that enhance our workflow and capabilities.

As a Smart India Hackathon winner and researcher planning to publish Live-In Lab research, I'm constantly exploring new technologies and methodologies to stay at the forefront of innovation in the gaming and immersive tech industries.
